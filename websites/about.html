About Lynux Caracal

Lynux Caracal is a high-performance, experimental operating system built for the next generation of computing. It features a modular architecture, a fast Lua-based kernel, and a sleek, modern interface.

Core Values

  • Speed: Optimized for low-latency interactions.
  • Privacy: Your data never leaves your local machine.
  • Customizability: Everything can be themed and modified.
